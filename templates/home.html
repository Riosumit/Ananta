why one should donate
be someone's hope, donate life
There is a wide gap between patients who need transplants and the organs that are available in India. An estimated around 1.8 lakh persons suffer from renal failure every year, however the number of renal transplants done is around 6000 only. An estimated 2 lac patients die of liver failure or liver cancer annually in India, about 10-15% of which can be saved with a timely liver transplant.

Hence about 25-30 thousand liver transplants are needed annually in India but only about one thousand five hundred are being performed. Similarly about 50000 persons suffer from Heart failures annually but only about 10 to 15 heart transplants are performed every year in India. In case of Cornea, about 25000 transplants are done every year against a requirement of 1 lakh.

WE WILL PROVIDE DISCOUNT AND OFFERS IN MEDICAL FACILITIES
Government hospitals are known for their skilled and experienced healthcare professionals, and we are proud to partner with them to offer our volunteers discounted rates for various medical procedures. This means that our volunteers can access high-quality medical care at a more affordable cost. In addition to discounted rates, government hospitals also offer separate time slots for medical checkups for our volunteers. This ensures that our volunteers won't have to wait in long lines or compete with other patients for medical attention.
